Caramel Tart

Scrumptious heavenly caramel tart

This is for those chocolate fiends who drool over good smelling deserts

Ingredients to make this divine desert

For the pastry

  • 190g plain flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 125g chilled unsalted butter, cubed
  • 50g (2oz) caster sugar
  • 1 egg

For the caramel layer

  • 375g caster sugar
  • 100g (3 1/2oz) golden syrup
  • 100g unsalted butter
  • 150ml double cream
  • pinch of sea salt

For the chocolate layer

  • 100ml (3 1/2fl oz) double cream
  • 150g (5oz) dark chocolate, chopped into small pieces
  • pinch of sea salt, for the top
