Lasagna

Yummy delicious Lasagna

Lasagna is a relatively easy to make dish and what's more it absolutely divine!

Ingredients need to make mouth watering lasagna

  • 2 olive oil, plus a little for the dish
  • 750g lean beef mince
  • 90g pack prosciutto
  • tomato sauce
  • 200ml hot beef stock
  • a little grated nutmeg
  • 300g fresh lasagne sheets
  • white sauce
  • 125g ball mozzarella, torn into thin strips
